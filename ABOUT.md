# About Pilgrim's Path

## Mission

Pilgrim's Path exists to make the sacred pilgrimages of Hajj and Umrah accessible to every Muslim on earth through immersive virtual reality technology. We believe that understanding and preparing for these sacred journeys should not be limited by physical ability, financial means, or geographic distance.

## The Problem We Solve

Every year, millions of Muslims perform Hajj and Umrah — but many more cannot:

- **2+ million** Muslims perform Hajj annually, but **millions more** are unable due to health, age, disability, or financial constraints
- **First-time pilgrims** often feel overwhelmed by the complexity of rituals they've never practiced
- **Islamic schools** lack immersive teaching tools for pilgrimage education
- **Travel agencies** struggle to prepare clients for what to expect
- **Elderly and disabled Muslims** may never experience the holy sites

## Our Solution

Pilgrim's Path provides a **free, browser-based, immersive VR platform** that:

1. **Teaches every Hajj ritual** — from Ihram preparation through Tawaf al-Wida, day by day
2. **Guides Umrah practice** — all four steps with authentic duas and visual cues
3. **Transports users to sacred sites** — 360° photorealistic views of Makkah, Madinah, and every Hajj/Umrah location
4. **Provides complete dua collections** — Arabic, transliteration, and English for every ritual moment
5. **Runs on any device** — phone, tablet, laptop, desktop, or VR headset — no download needed

## Content Coverage

### Hajj Virtual Experience (5 Days)
- **Day 1 (8th Dhul Hijjah)**: Ihram, Niyyah, Talbiyah, travel to Mina
- **Day 2 (9th Dhul Hijjah)**: Standing at Arafat (Wuquf), travel to Muzdalifah, collecting pebbles
- **Day 3 (10th Dhul Hijjah — Eid al-Adha)**: Rami Jamarat (stoning), Qurbani (sacrifice), Halq/Taqsir (shaving/trimming), Tawaf al-Ifadah, Sa'i
- **Days 4-5 (11th-12th Dhul Hijjah — Ayyam al-Tashreeq)**: Continued stoning of all three Jamarat
- **Farewell**: Tawaf al-Wida (Farewell Tawaf)

### Umrah Virtual Experience (4 Steps)
1. **Ihram**: Sacred state, Miqat, intentions, Talbiyah
2. **Tawaf**: Seven circuits around the Kaaba with duas
3. **Sa'i**: Walking between Safa and Marwah (7 times)
4. **Halq/Taqsir**: Shaving or trimming hair to exit sacred state

### Sacred Sites Explored
**Makkah**: Kaaba, Black Stone (Hajar al-Aswad), Maqam Ibrahim, Hateem (Hijr Ismail), Zamzam Well, Safa & Marwah, Masjid al-Haram, Mina, Arafat, Muzdalifah, Jamarat Bridge, Jabal al-Nour, Cave of Hira, Jabal Thawr

**Madinah**: Masjid al-Nabawi, Rawdah Sharif, Jannat al-Baqi, Masjid Quba

### Duas & Prayers
50+ authenticated supplications covering every ritual moment — Talbiyah, Tawaf circuits, Sa'i, Arafat, Muzdalifah, Jamarat, and general Hajj/Umrah prayers. All provided in Arabic script, transliteration, and English translation.
