Sacred Supplications

Duas & Prayers

Essential supplications for your Hajj and Umrah journey, with Arabic text, transliteration, and translation.

Dua (supplication) is the essence of worship. During Hajj and Umrah, there are special occasions when duas are particularly accepted. Learn these sacred prayers to enhance your spiritual journey.

Essential

The Talbiyah

Throughout Hajj & Umrah
لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ لَبَّيْكَ، إِنَّ الْحَمْدَ وَالنِّعْمَةَ لَكَ وَالْمُلْكَ، لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ
Transliteration: Labbayk Allāhumma labbayk, labbayk lā sharīka laka labbayk, inna al-ḥamda wan-ni'mata laka wal-mulk, lā sharīka lak
Translation: "Here I am, O Allah, here I am. Here I am, You have no partner, here I am. Verily all praise and blessings are Yours, and all sovereignty. You have no partner."
Sahih al-Bukhari

At the Black Stone

Beginning of Tawaf
بِسْمِ اللهِ، اللهُ أَكْبَرُ
Transliteration: Bismillāh, Allāhu Akbar
Translation: "In the name of Allah, Allah is the Greatest."
Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ

Between Rukn Yamani and Black Stone

During Each Circuit
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ
Transliteration: Rabbanā ātinā fid-dunyā ḥasanatan wa fil-ākhirati ḥasanatan wa qinā 'adhāban-nār
Translation: "Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire."
Quran 2:201

Upon Reaching Safa

Sa'i
إِنَّ الصَّفَا وَالْمَرْوَةَ مِنْ شَعَائِرِ اللهِ
Transliteration: Innaṣ-ṣafā wal-marwata min sha'ā'irillāh
Translation: "Indeed, Safa and Marwa are among the symbols of Allah."
Quran 2:158
Most Important

Best Dua of Arafah

Day of Arafah
لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
Transliteration: Lā ilāha illallāhu waḥdahu lā sharīka lah, lahul-mulku wa lahul-ḥamdu wa huwa 'alā kulli shay'in qadīr
Translation: "There is no god but Allah alone, with no partner. His is the dominion and His is the praise, and He has power over all things."
Tirmidhi

During Stoning (Rami)

At Each Pebble
اللهُ أَكْبَرُ
Transliteration: Allāhu Akbar
Translation: "Allah is the Greatest." (Said with each pebble thrown)
Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ

Seeking Forgiveness

Anytime
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي
Transliteration: Allāhumma innaka 'afuwwun tuḥibbul-'afwa fa'fu 'annī
Translation: "O Allah, You are Forgiving and love forgiveness, so forgive me."
Tirmidhi

When Drinking Zamzam

At Zamzam Well
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ عِلْمًا نَافِعًا، وَرِزْقًا وَاسِعًا، وَشِفَاءً مِنْ كُلِّ دَاءٍ
Transliteration: Allāhumma innī as'aluka 'ilman nāfi'an, wa rizqan wāsi'an, wa shifā'an min kulli dā'
Translation: "O Allah, I ask You for beneficial knowledge, plentiful provision, and healing from every disease."
Reported Dua
