## What is Pilgrim's Path?

Pilgrim's Path uses 360° photorealistic virtual reality to help Muslims worldwide prepare for, learn about, and spiritually connect with the sacred pilgrimages of **Hajj** and **Umrah**. The entire platform is **free** — no subscription, no paywall. It works in any modern browser with no download required.

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Virtual Hajj** | Walk through all 5 days of Hajj in immersive VR — Ihram, Tawaf, Sa'i, Mina, Arafat, Muzdalifah, Rami Jamarat, Qurbani, Halq/Taqsir, Tawaf al-Ifadah, Farewell Tawaf |
| **Virtual Umrah** | Practice all 4 steps — Ihram, Tawaf, Sa'i, Halq/Taqsir — in 360° environments |
| **360° Sacred Sites** | Explore Masjid al-Haram, Kaaba, Masjid al-Nabawi, and 20+ landmarks in Makkah & Madinah |
| **Duas Library** | 50+ essential supplications in Arabic, transliteration, and English |
| **Interactive Scenes** | Animated Ihram preparation, barber (Halq/Taqsir), Qurbani sacrifice, and more |
| **Enterprise Solutions** | White-label VR for mosques, schools, travel agencies, hospitals |

## Who Is This For?

- 🕋 **First-time pilgrims** preparing for Hajj or Umrah
- 👴 **Elderly Muslims** who cannot physically travel
- ♿ **Disabled Muslims** seeking spiritual connection
- 🏫 **Islamic schools** teaching Hajj/Umrah curricula
- 🕌 **Mosques** running pre-trip preparation programs
- ✈️ **Travel agencies** training clients before departure
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 **Families** teaching children about pilgrimage
- 🤲 **Revert/new Muslims** learning about the fifth pillar of Islam
- 🏥 **Hospitals & care homes** offering spiritual experiences

## Technology

- **Platform**: Progressive web app (browser-based)
- **VR Engine**: 3DVista VR Runtime, WebXR
- **Media**: High-res 360° panoramic photography of actual holy sites
- **Audio**: Spatial audio with Quran recitation and ambient sounds
- **Devices**: Phone, tablet, desktop, Meta Quest, any VR headset with browser
- **No Download**: Works instantly in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge
