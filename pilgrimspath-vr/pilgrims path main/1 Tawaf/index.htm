Step 1

Cleansing & Garments

✦
  • Perform Ghusl (full ritual bath) or at minimum Wudu
  • For men: Wear two white unstitched cloths — the Izaar (lower body) and the Rida (over the shoulder)
  • For women: Wear modest, loose clothing covering the body; your face and hands remain uncovered
  • You may apply fragrance to your body (not the garments) before making your intention

Step 2

Your Ihram & Niyyah

✦

How the Ihram garments are worn

⚠ Ihram Restrictions

Once in the state of Ihram, observe these restrictions:

✦Men: two seamless white garments only
✦Women: modest clothing covering the body
✦No perfume or scented products
✦No cutting hair or nails
✦No marital relations
✦No hunting animals
✦No disputes or arguments

Your Niyyah at the Miqat

You must make your Niyyah before or at the Miqat boundary The Miqat is the sacred boundary you cross on your journey — once past it, you must already be in the state of Ihram

Declare your intention for Umrah or Hajj clearly:

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أُرِيدُ الْعُمْرَةَ فَيَسِّرْهَا لِي وَتَقَبَّلْهَا مِنِّي

"Allahumma inni ureedal Umrata fa yassirha li wa taqabbalha minni"

"O Allah, I intend to perform Umrah, so make it easy for me and accept it from me."
