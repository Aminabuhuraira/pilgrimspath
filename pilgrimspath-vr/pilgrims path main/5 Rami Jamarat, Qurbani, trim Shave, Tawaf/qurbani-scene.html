Qurbani — The Sacred Sacrifice

📋
What is Qurbani?

The ritual sacrifice of a livestock animal (sheep, goat, cow, or camel) performed on the 10th, 11th, or 12th of Dhul Hijjah. It commemorates Prophet Ibrahim's (AS) willingness to sacrifice his son.

📜
Key Rules

• Animal must be healthy and of proper age
• Must say Bismillah, Allahu Akbar before slaughter
• Meat divided into 3 parts: family, relatives, and the needy
• Can be delegated to a trusted agent
