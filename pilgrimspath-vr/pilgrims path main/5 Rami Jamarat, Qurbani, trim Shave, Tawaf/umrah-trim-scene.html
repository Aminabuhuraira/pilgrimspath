Step 3 of 16 · End of Umrah

Halq or Taqsir — Completing Umrah

💈
Halq
حَلْق

Men: complete head shave (more virtuous). The Prophet ﷺ made dua thrice for those who shave.

✂️
Taqsir
تَقْصِير

Trim at least a fingertip's length. Required option for women.

You now exit the state of Ihram. All Ihram restrictions are lifted. You will rest and prepare for the 8th of Dhul-Hijjah to begin Hajj.

🎉 Congratulations — Umrah Complete

You have completed Tawaf, Sa‘i, and Halq/Taqsir. Now rest and await the start of Hajj.

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِلْمُحَلِّقِينَ وَالْمُقَصِّرِينَ

"Allāhumma-ghfir lil-muḥalliqīna wal-muqaṣṣirīn"

"O Allah, forgive those who shave their heads and those who trim their hair."
