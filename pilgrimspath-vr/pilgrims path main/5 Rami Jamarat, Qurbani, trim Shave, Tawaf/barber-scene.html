Barber Scene — Trim / Shave (Halq & Taqsir)
✂️

Step 8 of Hajj

Halq or Taqsir — Hair Trimming

💈
Halq
حَلْق

Complete head shaving — considered the more virtuous option. The Prophet ﷺ made dua thrice for those who shave.

✂️
Taqsir
تَقْصِير

Trimming at least a fingertip's length of hair from all over the head. Required option for women.

After trimming you exit the state of Ihram — all restrictions of Ihram are now lifted.

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِلْمُحَلِّقِينَ وَالْمُقَصِّرِينَ

"Allahumma-ghfir lil-muhalliqeena wal-muqasssireen"

"O Allah, forgive those who shave their heads and those who trim their hair."
