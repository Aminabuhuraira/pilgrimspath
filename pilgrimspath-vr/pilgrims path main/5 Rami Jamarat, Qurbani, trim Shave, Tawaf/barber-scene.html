Step 11 of 16 · Day of Sacrifice

Halaq — Shaving the Head

🪒
Halq
حَلْق

Complete head shaving — considered the more virtuous option. The Prophet ﷺ made dua thrice for those who shave.

✂️
Taqsir
تَقْصِير

Trimming at least a fingertip's length of hair from all over the head. Required option for women.

This is the partial exit from Ihram (Tahallul al-Awwal). All restrictions are lifted except marital relations — those resume only after Tawaf al-Ifadah.

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِلْمُحَلِّقِينَ وَالْمُقَصِّرِينَ

"Allahumma-ghfir lil-muhalliqeena wal-muqassireen"

"O Allah, forgive those who shave their heads and those who trim their hair."
