Step 6 of 18 — Rest Between Umrah & Hajj

Resting & Praying

Voice-over Alhamdulillah — your Umrah is complete. Between now and the start of Hajj on the 8th of Dhul-Hijjah, you are temporarily released from the state of Ihram. Use this sacred pause wisely: rest your body, increase your prayers in Al-Masjid Al-Haram, recite Qur'an, and prepare your heart for the demanding days ahead. The greatest worship in this time is Tawaf, prayer in the Haram, and remembrance of Allah.

Recommended Acts of Worship

  • Pray as many obligatory and voluntary prayers as possible in the Haram
  • Perform additional Tawaf — every step is multiplied in reward
  • Recite Qur'an, especially Surah al-Baqarah, al-Imran, and al-Kahf
  • Make abundant du'a while gazing at the Kaaba
  • Drink Zamzam with the intention of healing and barakah
  • Rest, hydrate, and conserve strength for the days ahead
  • Reconnect with family back home and ask for their du'a
