Entering Al-Masjid Al-Haram

Enter with your right foot, with humility and reverence, and recite:

✦

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ، اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ، اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي ذُنُوبِي وَافْتَحْ لِي أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ

Bismillahi, Allahumma salli ala Muhammadin wa ali Muhammad. Allahumma ighfir li dhunubi wa iftah li abwaba rahmatika.

'In the name of Allah, O Allah, send prayers upon Muhammad and his family. O Allah, forgive my sins and open the gates of Your mercy.'

✦
