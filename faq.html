General Questions

What is Pilgrim's Path?

Pilgrim's Path is an immersive virtual reality platform designed to help Muslims learn about and experience the sacred pilgrimages of Hajj and Umrah. Using cutting-edge VR technology, we provide an educational and spiritually enriching experience that helps pilgrims prepare for their journey or allows those unable to travel to experience the beauty of these rituals virtually.

Is Pilgrim's Path really free?

Yes, absolutely! Pilgrim's Path is 100% free and will remain free forever. We believe that knowledge about Islamic worship and pilgrimage should be accessible to everyone. We are funded through donations and grants from Islamic organizations and philanthropists who share our vision.

Is the content authentic and verified?

All content on Pilgrim's Path is carefully reviewed and verified by qualified Islamic scholars. Our advisory board includes graduates from renowned Islamic institutions including Al-Azhar University, Islamic University of Madinah, and others. We follow authentic Sunnah and mainstream Islamic jurisprudence.

Who is Mufti Menk and why is he involved?

Mufti Ismail Menk is a renowned Islamic scholar from Zimbabwe who has inspired millions of Muslims worldwide through his lectures, social media presence, and compassionate approach to Islamic education. He serves as our brand ambassador, endorsing our mission to make pilgrimage education accessible to all Muslims.

VR Experience

Do I need a VR headset to use Pilgrim's Path?

No! While we offer the best experience with VR headsets, our platform is designed to work on:

  • Desktop/Laptop: Full 360° panorama with mouse controls
  • Mobile devices: Gyroscope-based viewing and touch controls
  • VR Headsets: Full immersive experience (Meta Quest, HTC Vive, etc.)
What VR devices are supported?

We support all major VR platforms including Meta Quest 2 & 3, HTC Vive, Valve Index, PlayStation VR, and Windows Mixed Reality headsets. Our mobile experience also works with Google Cardboard and similar phone-based VR viewers.

How realistic is the VR experience?

Our VR experience uses high-resolution 360° photography and video from the actual holy sites, combined with 3D modeling for educational walkthroughs. While nothing can replace the spiritual experience of being physically present, our platform provides an authentic visual and educational representation of the pilgrimage.

Religious Questions

Does VR Hajj/Umrah count as the real thing?

No, absolutely not. Pilgrim's Path is purely an educational and preparatory tool. Virtual pilgrimage does NOT fulfill the religious obligation of Hajj or Umrah. There is no substitute for the actual physical pilgrimage. Our platform is designed to:

  • Help future pilgrims prepare and learn the rituals
  • Provide education for those who haven't yet had the opportunity
  • Allow elderly or disabled individuals to learn about the experience
  • Refresh knowledge for those who have already performed pilgrimage
Is it permissible (halal) to use this platform?

Yes, using educational technology to learn about Islamic practices is permissible. Our platform has been reviewed by qualified scholars who have confirmed that using VR for educational purposes about Hajj and Umrah is not only permissible but praiseworthy when it leads to better understanding and preparation for actual pilgrimage.

Which madhab (school of thought) do you follow?

We present pilgrimage rituals according to authentic Sunnah with references to all four major Sunni schools of thought (Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, and Hanbali). Where there are differences of opinion, we mention them clearly so users can follow their own madhab or consult their local scholars.

Creating an account is simple and free! Click the "Start Free Journey" or "Create Account" button on our homepage. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google account for faster registration.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes, we take privacy very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.

What are the system requirements?

For basic access (360° viewing), you only need a modern web browser. For the full VR experience, we recommend:

  • VR Ready PC: Intel i5/AMD Ryzen 5+, 8GB RAM, GTX 1060+
  • Standalone VR: Meta Quest 2 or newer
  • Mobile: iOS 14+ or Android 10+
  • Internet: 10 Mbps+ for smooth streaming
