Our Story

About Pilgrim's Path

Bridging technology and spirituality to make the sacred pilgrimage experience accessible to Muslims worldwide.

Our Mission

Making Sacred Knowledge Accessible

Pilgrim's Path was born from a simple yet profound question: How can we help Muslims around the world prepare for the journey of a lifetime?

For many Muslims, performing Hajj or Umrah remains a distant dream due to financial constraints, health issues, or limited quotas. Even for those blessed with the opportunity, the complexity of rituals and the overwhelming experience can be daunting.

We created Pilgrim's Path to bridge this gap — using cutting-edge VR technology to provide an immersive, educational, and spiritually enriching experience that prepares pilgrims for their sacred journey and allows others to experience its beauty virtually.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Sincerity (Ikhlas)

Everything we do is for the pleasure of Allah and the benefit of the Ummah. Our platform is free because knowledge of worship should never be monetized.

Authenticity

All content is verified by qualified Islamic scholars to ensure accuracy according to authentic Sunnah and mainstream Islamic jurisprudence.

Accessibility

We believe every Muslim deserves access to quality pilgrimage education regardless of their location, language, or economic status.

Innovation

We leverage the latest technology to create meaningful spiritual experiences while respecting the sacred traditions of Islam.
