**1\. Welcome**

Welcome to this immersive Hajj experience, a journey of faith, devotion, and unity. Together, we will explore the sacred rites, duas, and profound meanings of Hajj, bringing you closer to the essence of Hajj.

May Allah accept your intentions and grant you blessings as we walk this path together.

---

**2\. Ihram (State of Spiritual Purity)**

* **Voice-over:** "Your journey begins with entering the state of Ihram. Before putting on Ihram garments, you need to observe several restrictions:  
  1. Men must wear only two seamless white garments. Women should wear modest clothing that covers their entire body.  
  2. Do not apply perfume or any scented products.  
  3. Avoid cutting your hair or nails.  
  4. Refrain from marital relations.  
  5. Hunting or killing animals is prohibited.  
  6. Avoid disputes or arguments.

---

3\.

After donning Ihram, make the intention for Hajj and recite the Talbiyah:

*'Labbayka Allahumma labbayk, labbayka la sharika laka labbayk. Inna al-hamda wa an-ni'mata laka wal-mulk, la sharika lak.'*

Translation: 'Here I am, O Allah, here I am. Here I am, You have no partner, here I am. Verily, all praise, grace, and sovereignty belong to You, and You have no partner.'

---

**4\. Congratulations**

You are now in the state of Ihram and must adhere to these restrictions."

---

**5\. Entering the Haram Mosque**

* **Voice-over:** "As you approach the sacred mosque, Al-Masjid Al-Haram, enter with humility and reverence. Step forward with your right foot and recite:

    
  *'Bismillahi, Allahumma salli ala Muhammadin wa ali Muhammad. Allahumma ighfir li dhunubi wa iftah li abwaba rahmatika.'*  
  Translation: 'In the name of Allah, O Allah, send prayers upon Muhammad and his family. O Allah, forgive my sins and open the gates of Your mercy.'"

---

**6\. Dua upon Seeing the Kaaba for the First Time**

* **Voice-over:** "Upon citing the Kaaba, raise your hands and make any personal dua you wish. This is a moment when your dua is especially powerful. You may also recite:

    
  *'Allahumma zid hadha al-bayt tashreefan wa ta'dheeman wa takreeman wa mahabatan, wa zid man sharrafahu wa karramahu mimman hajjahu awi'tamarahu tashreefan wa takreeman wa ta'dheeman wa birran.'*

    
  Translation: 'O Allah, increase this house (the Kaaba) in honor, nobility, and respect, and increase in honor and nobility the one who honors and respects it.'"

---

**7\. Tawaf (Circumambulation of the Kaaba)**

* **Voice-over:** "You will now begin Tawaf, circling the Kaaba seven times. Starting from the Black Stone, raise your hand towards it, and say 

  *'Bismillahi Allahu Akbar'* (In the name of Allah, Allah is the Greatest).

---

8\. You have now begone your Tawaf, you may recite any duas or dhikr that you find meaningful. 

---

**9\. When passing between the Yemeni Corner and the Black Stone, recite:**

*'Rabbana atina fi dunya hasanatan wa fil akhirati hasanatan wa qina adhaban-nar.'*

Translation: 'Our Lord, give us in this world that which is good, and in the Hereafter that which is good, and save us from the torment of the Fire.'"

---

**10\. Praying at Maqam Ibrahim**

* **Voice-over:** "Here at the Maqam Ibrahim (the Station of Ibrahim), you may offer two rak’ahs of Salah. In the first rak’ah, recite Surah Al-Fatiha followed by Surah Al-Kafirun. In the second rak’ah, recite Surah Al-Fatiha followed by Surah Al-Ikhlas. After completing the prayer, make any personal supplications."

---

**11\. Sa’i Between Safa and Marwa**

* **Voice-over:** "You will now perform Sa’i, walking between Safa and Marwa. Begin at Safa, facing the Kaaba, and recite:

    
  *'Inna as-safa wal-marwata min sha'a'irillah.'*

    
  Translation: 'Indeed, Safa and Marwa are among the symbols of Allah.'

  You may also recite :

  Abda’u bimā badā’ Llāhu bih.

  Translation :I begin with that which Allah has begun with

  La ilāha illa Llāhu waḥdahu la sharīka lah, lahu l-mulku wa lahu l-ḥamd, yuḥyī wa yumīt, wa huwa ʿalā kulli shay’in qadīr.

  Translation : There is no deity except Allah, alone without a partner. To Him belongs the Dominion, and to Him belongs all praise. He gives life and death, and He has power over everything.

  La ilāha illa Llāhu waḥdah, anjaza waʿdahu wa naṣara ʿabdahu wa hazama l-aḥzāba waḥdah.

  Translation: There is no deity except Allah alone. He fulfilled His promise, supported His slave and defeated the Confederates alone.

  ---

  12\. As you walk between the two hills seven times. Recite dhikr and personal duas. When you reach the green markers, hasten your pace (men only), then resume walking at a normal pace after passing them. Complete Sa’i by reaching Marwa for the seventh time." 

---

**13\. Halaq Or Qasar.**

	Voice over :Upon completing Saee, Men get their heads shaved or trimmed , while 	women only get their hair trimmed.

This now signifies you are out of the boundaries of Ihram and will await the 8th of Dhul Hajjah.

---

**14\. Congratulations**

You have now completed Umrah

---

**15\. 8th Day of Dhul-Hijjah (Yawm at-Tarwiyah) ( MINA )**

* **Voice-over:** "On this day the 8th day of Dhul-Hijjah, known as Yawm at-Tarwiyah, You have arrived at Mina where you will spend the day in worship, arrival is before noon.   
  * You will Offer the following prayers, but some are shortened to two rak’ahs the likes of, Zuhr, Asr, & Isha.

  Throughout the day, continue reciting the Talbiyah and prepare for the Day of Arafah."

---

**16\. 9th Day of Dhul-Hijjah (Day of Arafah)**

* **Voice-over:** "On this day the 9th day, the Day of Arafah we have now arrived on the plains of Arafat. Walk your way to the summit as this is a pivotal moment, where your supplications are answered.

---

17\.	You will now be taken to Muzdalifah. Here, pray Maghrib and Isha together, 	shortened and combined, with one Adhan and two Iqamahs. At Muzdalifah go collect 	at least 49 or 70 pebbles for the stoning ritual for the next day.

---

 

18\.	Congratulations, you may now procced to any resting area under the open sky."

---

**19\. 10th Day of Dhul-Hijjah (Yawm an-Nahr \- The Day of Sacrifice)**  
• Voice-over: "Today is the 10th day, Yawm an-Nahr. After completing Fajr prayer, we have now arrived at Rami al-jamarat where we will perform four key actions:

* **Rami al-Jamarat**  
* **Sacrifice (Qurbani)**  
* **Shaving or Trimming the Hair**  
* **Travel back to Al-Masjid Al-Haram in Makkah**

---

**20\. 	1\. Rami al-Jamarat (Stoning of the Pillars):** You will now begin by stoning Jamarat al-Aqabah with seven pebbles, reciting 'Bismillahi Allahu Akbar' with each throw.

---

**21\.	2\. Sacrifice (Qurbani):** Make your way to the merchant to perform a sacrificial offering.

By offering a token, they will handle the Qurbani and give out the meat as sadaqa in your name to those most in need.

---

**22\.	3\. Shaving or Trimming the Hair:** Here Men will shave their heads or trim their hair, while women will cut a small portion of their hair.

---

**23\.**	You will now proceed to travel back to Al-Masjid Al-Haram within Makkah.

---

**24\.	Al-Masjid Al-Haram:** this is the second tawaf you will perform, the first one being the one carried out during umrah, this tawaf is called  Tawaf Al-Ifadah "

---

25\.	Now, you’ll journey back to Mina to complete the Rami Al-Jamarat rituals for the 11th, 12th, and optional for the 13th day of Hajj

---

### **26\.	11th Day of Dhul-Hijjah**

### Voice Over: As you begin the **11th Day of Dhul-Hijjah you will be repeating the stoning of the Rami al-Jamarat :**

### **Carry out the stoning in the following sequence:**

* **Jamarat al-Ula** (first pillar)  
  * **Jamarat al-Wusta** (middle pillar)  
    * **Jamarat al-Aqabah** (last and largest pillar)  
  * For each pillar:  
    * Throw **seven pebbles** one by one while saying **“Bismillahi Allahu Akbar”** with each throw.

---

**27\.** 	This completes the stoning ritual for the 11th day.

---

### **28\.	12th Day of Dhul-Hijjah**

* Once again, you’re at Mina and ready to perform the stoning ritual of Rami Al jamarat on the 12th day of dhul Hijjah  
  * For each pillar:  
    * Throw **seven pebbles** at each, saying **“Bismillahi Allahu Akbar”** with every throw.  
    * **Jamarat al-Ula**  
    * **Jamarat al-Wusta**  
    * **Jamarat al-Aqabah**

---

**29\.**	**Decision to Stay or Leave Mina**:

Well-done on completing the stoning ritual on this day, you have the choice to either:  
Leave Mina to perform your final tawaf early.  
or  
Remain in Mina to continue the ritual on the 13th day.

---

### **30\.	13th Day of Dhul-Hijjah (if you choose to stay)**

1. **Final Day in Mina**:  
   * If you stayed, this is your last day to complete the rites of Tashreeq.  
2. **Rami al-Jamarat (Stoning of the Pillars)**:  
   * Once more, visit each of the three pillars in the following order:  
     * **Jamarat al-Ula**  
     * **Jamarat al-Wusta**  
     * **Jamarat al-Aqabah**  
   * For each pillar:  
     * Throw **seven pebbles** while saying **“Bismillahi Allahu Akbar”** with each throw.

---

**31\.	Depart from Mina**:  
Congratulations on completing the stoning on this final day, You will now leave Mina for Masjidul-Haram, as you have completed the rites of Tashreeq

---

**32\.	Farewell Tawaf (Tawaf al-Wada’)**

* **Voice-over:** "This is considered as the farewell tawaf (Tawaf al- wada)

---

**33\.**	As you have completed the Tawaf, exit Al-Masjid Al-Haram with your right foot and recite:

*'Bismillahi wa s-salatu wa s-salamu ‘ala rasulillah, Allahumma inni as’aluka min fadlika.'*

Translation: 'In the name of Allah, and prayers and peace upon the Messenger of Allah. O Allah, I ask You for Your bounty.'"

Congratulations on completing your hajj.

May Allah accept this act of ibadah that you have performed.
